Event name: scrollStart

Triggered when you begin scrolling through a chart.

All events, when triggered, will provide two parameters for the handler - eventObj (object containing information generic to all events) and dataObj (object containing information specific to an event).

Use the scroll bar to scroll through the chart. The scrollStart event is triggered just when you start scrolling. Scroll down to the table rendered below the chart to view information contained in the dataObj object. To view information contained in the eventObj object, open the console.
