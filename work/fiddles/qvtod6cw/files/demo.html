Event name: connectorRollover

Triggered when the mouse pointer is rolled over a connector connecting the tasks (on a Gantt chart) or the markers (on a map).

All events, when triggered, will provide two parameters for the handler - eventObj (object containing information generic to all events) and dataObj (object containing information specific to an event).

Roll the mouse pointer over a connector to trigger the event. Scroll down to the table rendered below the chart to view information contained in the dataObj object. To view information contained in the eventObj object, open the console.
