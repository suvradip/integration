Method Name: resetChart()

Resets all the zooming, panning, and pinning actions performed on a zoom line chart.

To enable the Reset button, zoom in to the zoom-line chart shown below (or switch to the pin mode using the pin button at the top-right corner). Now, click the Reset button to zoom out one level at a time (or to switch to the zoom mode from the pin mode).

The button is again disabled when the chart is reset to its original dimensions or to the zoom mode.
