Event name: beforeResize

Triggered before a chart is resized.

All events, when triggered, will provide two parameters for the handler - eventObj (object containing information generic to all events) and dataObj (object containing information specific to an event).

Click the '+' or '-' buttons (for width/height) below the chart to trigger the event. Scroll down to the table rendered below the chart to view information contained in the dataObj object. To view information contained in the eventObj object, open the console.
