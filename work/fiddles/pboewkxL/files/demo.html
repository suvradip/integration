Event name: legendRangeUpdated

Triggered when the range of a gradient legend, in the heat map chart and maps, is updated by dragging the legend pointers.

All events, when triggered, will provide two parameters for the handler - eventObj (object containing information generic to all events) and dataObj (object containing information specific to an event).

Drag a legend pointer to update the range and trigger the event. Scroll down to the table rendered below the chart to view information contained in the dataObj object. To view information contained in the eventObj object, open the console.
