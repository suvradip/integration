Method Name: zoomOut()

Zooms out the zoom line chart by one level

To enable the Zoom Out button, you will first have to zoom in to the chart. Press the left mouse button and drag the mouse pointer across the zoom line chart to zoom it. Now, click the Zoom Out button to zoom out the chart by one level.
The button is again disabled when the zoom-line chart is zoomed out to its original dimensions.
