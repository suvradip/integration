Event name: milestoneRollout

Triggered when the mouse pointer is rolled out from over a milestone on a Gantt chart.

All events, when triggered, will provide two parameters for the handler - eventObj (object containing information generic to all events) and dataObj (object containing information specific to an event).

Roll the mouse pointer over and out of a milestone on a Gantt chart to trigger the event. Scroll down to the table rendered below the chart to view information contained in the dataObj object. To view information contained in the eventObj object, open the console.
