Event name: dataLabelRollOver

Triggered when the mouse pointer is rolled over the x-axis label of a data plot.

All events, when triggered, will provide two parameters for the handler - eventObj (object containing information generic to all events) and dataObj (object containing information specific to an event).

Roll the mouse pointer over any of the x-axis labels (Jan, Feb, Mar, and so on) to trigger the dataLabelRollOver event. Scroll down to the table rendered below the chart to view information contained in the dataObj object. To view information contained in the eventObj object, open the console.
