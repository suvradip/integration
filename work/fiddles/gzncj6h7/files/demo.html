Event name: beforeDataSubmit

Triggered when the Submit button on interactive charts (select-scatter, drag-node, and drag-able charts) is clicked, after the data values are updated by the user.

All events, when triggered, will provide two parameters for the handler - eventObj (object containing information generic to all events) and dataObj (object containing information specific to an event).

Select a subset of data on the chart. Click Submit to trigger the event. Scroll down to the table rendered below the chart to view information contained in the dataObj object. To view information contained in the eventObj object, open the console.
