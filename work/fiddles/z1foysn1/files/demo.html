Event name: slicingStart

Triggered when a pie or a doughnut slice begins slicing out/slicing in.

All events, when triggered, will provide two parameters for the handler - eventObj (object containing information generic to all events) and dataObj (object containing information specific to an event).

Click any pie slice to slice it out. The slicingStart event is triggered just when the pie slice begins to slice out. Scroll down to the table rendered below the chart to view information contained in the dataObj object. To view information contained in the eventObj object, open the console.
