Event name: rotationStart

Triggered when a pie/doughnut chart starts rotating.

All events, when triggered, will provide two parameters for the handler - eventObj (object containing information generic to all events) and dataObj (object containing information specific to an event).

Press and drag the left mouse pointer to rotate the pie chart and trigger the event. Scroll down to the table rendered below the chart to view information contained in the dataObj object. To view information contained in the eventObj object, open the console.
